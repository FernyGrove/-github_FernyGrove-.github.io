    What is Dungeons and Dragons?

    Dungeons & Dragons is a tabletop roleplaying game that takes you and your friends on epic journeys. Become characters you create, battle deadly foes, uncover secrets, find treasure, and make memories that last a lifetime.

    What kind of DnD experience do y'all offer?

    We've been playing 5e for about 3 years now and are glad to show anyone who's willing to play a good time. We've run about 7 differrent campaigns ranging from 1st level to 20th level. We can run homebrews, official content, one shots, etc. The themes and aesthics of each adventure varies from session to session. We can do serious stories or fun adventures. The sky is the limit when you play with friends!
